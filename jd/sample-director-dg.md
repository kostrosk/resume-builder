Director, Data Governance

About the role

We are seeking a Director of Data Governance to establish and lead the enterprise
data governance program. This leader will define the target operating model, chair
the Data Governance Council, and translate policy into executable technical controls
across our cloud data platform.

Responsibilities

- Define and own the enterprise data governance strategy, roadmap, and operating model
- Establish and chair a Data Governance Council; drive decision rights and accountability
- Build and maintain the enterprise data catalog and business glossary
- Lead data classification and sensitivity labeling across Snowflake, including tagging,
  masking policies, and row access policies for PII and regulated data
- Implement data quality monitoring, profiling, and remediation for critical data elements
- Own data lineage and metadata management to support impact analysis and audit readiness
- Partner with Legal, Privacy, Security, and Compliance on GDPR, CCPA, and SOX requirements
- Establish a data stewardship program; identify, train, and enable data stewards
- Drive adoption and data literacy through enablement, training, and change management
- Present program status and risk to executive leadership and the board

Required qualifications

- 10+ years in data management with 5+ years leading data governance
- Demonstrated experience standing up a data governance program from inception
- Hands-on experience with Snowflake, Power BI, and modern ELT tooling (dbt, Airflow)
- Experience with data catalog platforms (Collibra, Alation, OpenMetadata, or Purview)
- Strong background in data quality frameworks and metrics
- Proven executive communication and cross-functional stakeholder management
- Experience with master data management and reference data

Preferred

- Experience with AI governance and responsible AI frameworks
- DAMA CDMP or DCAM certification
- Experience in a regulated or public company environment
